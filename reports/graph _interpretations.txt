1. Distribution of Sale Price
   The distribution of SalePrice is positively skewed, indicating that most houses are moderately priced while a
   few luxury houses have much higher prices. The presence of high-priced outliers suggests that a logarithmic transformation
   of the target variable may improve model performance.

2. Missing Values in Each column
   Several columns contain missing values. Features such as PoolQC, Alley, Fence, and FireplaceQu have a very high percentage of 
   missing entries, which likely indicate the absence of those amenities rather than missing data. Columns like LotFrontage and 
   Electrical have relatively few missing values and can be imputed using statistical methods such as the median or mode.

| Column         | Decision                                                           |
